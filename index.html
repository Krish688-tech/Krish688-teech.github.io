Google Top Best SEO Tools

1․Google Page speed insights

Page Speed Insights (PSI) reports your page speed score and suggestions to make your website faster through our online tool and shows detailed information about performance of the page speed on your website on both mobile and desktop devices, and gives suggestions on how that a page need to be improved, that provides information about your site has performed based on data from your actual users around the world.

Why page speed importance ?

  • Longer page load times have a severe effect on bounce rate for example.
  • If page load time increase from 1 second to 3 seconds, bounces rate increases 32%.
  • If page load time increases form 1 second to 6 seconds, bounce rate increases by 106%.

Google Page Speed reports

1․Core web vitals assessment

Core web vitals reports based on three important metrics called LCP, FID, and CLS

Largest contentful Paint (LCP) Good score -2.5s or less.

The LCP reports the loading time of largest images or text block in your webpage.

First Input Delay (FID) Good Score – 100milliseconds or less.

The FID measures user’s experience and user’s interference and site responsiveness.

Cumulative Layout Shift(CLS) Good Score – 0.1 or less.

The CLS measures the largest burst of layout shift that means a visible element changes its position from one frame to another frame it occurs any time due to codding errors.

The 75 percentiles of all three metrics are considers good webpage in core web vitals assessment

2․Performance Score

A score of 90 or above is considered good,50 to 90 is a score that needs improvement, and below 50 is considered poor.

Finally PSI shows complete diagnose issues about a site that need to be fixed that issues like removed unused third party javascript library, unused CSS, large image and video files to improve your site performance.

4․Google Search Console

Google search console is helps you maintain and troubleshoot your sites presence in google search results you don’t have to sign up for search console to included in google search results.
Google search console tools helps you measure your site search traffic and performance and helps you understand and improve how google see your site.
Find and fix problem that shows google search engine traffic data for your site that reports how many times your site appears in google search that allows to check indexing status and optimize visibility of your websites and give alerts when google encounters indexing spam or any other issue on your site and trouble issue for AMP (googles Accelerated mobile pages), mobile usability and other search features.

Google Search Console reports

Impressions: The number of times any URL from your site appeared in search results viewed by a user not including paid google ads search impressions.
Clicks: The number of clicks on your website URLs from a google search results page not including clicks on paid google ads search results.
Average position: The average ranking of your website URL for the users searched keywords.
CTR: Click through rate calculated as clicks/impressions * 100.

4․Google Lighthouse

Lighthouse is an open-source, automated tool for improving the quality of web pages. You can run it against any web page. It has audits for performance, accessibility, progressive web apps, SEO and more.
Each audit has a reference doc explaining why the report is important, as well as how to fix it.

Run Lighthouse in Chrome DevTools

  • Download Google Chrome.
  • In Google Chrome, go to the any URL you want to audit.
  • Open Chrome DevTools.
  • Click the Lighthouse tab.
  • Click Generate Report. DevTools shows you a list of audit categories. Leave them all enabled.
  • After 30 to 60 seconds, Lighthouse gives you a report on the page.
